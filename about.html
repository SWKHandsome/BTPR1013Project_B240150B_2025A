Summary

Cybersecurity is an essential aspect of our digital lives that we must take seriously. As technologoy continues to evolve, so do the threats posed by cybercriminals. It's crucial to stay vigilant, proactive, and informed about the latest threats to protect our personal and professional information.

As the quote, "One should never intend to do harm to others, but should always guard against the harm others might do to him", Everyone should have the knowledge to protect themselves and their family from being harmed by others!

Let's begin with awareness. We hope this platform empowers you to make smarter and safer decisions online. Together, we can build a safer internet for all.
